Description & Process

At Kayode's Cocoa, we celebrate the journey of cocoa from its humble beginnings on sustainable farms in Nigeria to a refined, premium product that delights chocolate lovers and culinary professionals alike. Our commitment to ethical sourcing, quality production, and community empowerment is evident in every step of our operation, ensuring that each cocoa product reflects our dedication to excellence and sustainability.

Our process begins with directly sourcing the finest cocoa beans from local farmers who adhere to sustainable cultivation practices. Once harvested, the beans undergo a careful selection process—being sorted, cleaned, and fermented to develop their rich, natural flavor profiles. They are then sun-dried and roasted at controlled temperatures to preserve their aroma and integrity. After roasting, the beans are cracked and winnowed to separate the nibs from the shells, and the nibs are finely ground into cocoa mass. This cocoa mass is further processed into cocoa powder, cocoa butter, or custom blends, with every stage closely monitored through rigorous quality checks. To meet the diverse needs of our clients, we offer tailored blending and packaging solutions that elevate your cocoa experience. Our expert team collaborates with customers to customize flavor profiles and product formats, ensuring a perfect match for your culinary or manufacturing requirements. A final quality assurance check precedes each shipment, guaranteeing that our cocoa products consistently deliver exceptional performance and taste—from farm to table.
